We consider the following generic optimization problem

$$ \hat{x} = \underset{x}{\mathrm{argmin}} f(x) + g(x) $$

where both $$f$$ and $$g$$ are proper lower semi-continuous functions. In addition, we suppose that $$f$$ is $$\mu$$-smooth with respect to some Legendre function $$\phi$$.
